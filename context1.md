# Inside the Growth Engine of TBC Uzbekistan

TBC Uzbekistan operates one of the fastest-growing digital financial ecosystems in Central Asia.

The ecosystem includes two main products:

**TBC UZ** – a digital bank offering loans, deposits, and cards.
**Payme UZ** – a leading payments and financial services app.

Behind these products is a single growth organization: **Digital Acquisition**.

Our mission is simple.

Help more people discover, trust, and use modern financial services.

---

# One Growth Team. Many Channels.

The Digital Acquisition team works like an **internal growth agency**.

Instead of each product building its own marketing team, we operate one centralized unit that supports every business line.

This lets us move faster, test more ideas, and scale what works.

We manage the entire user journey:

**Discovery → Install → Activation → Product usage → Retention**

Every part of this funnel is owned by the same team.

---

# Paid Growth

Performance marketing helps us introduce our products to millions of potential users.

We run campaigns across major digital platforms and continuously test:

• creative formats
• audiences
• bidding strategies
• landing experiences

The goal is simple: bring the right users into the ecosystem.

---

# Lifecycle Communication

Growth does not stop after someone installs the app.

We run large-scale lifecycle programs using:

• push notifications
• SMS campaigns
• in-app messages

These help users discover new features, complete onboarding, and get value from our products.

Automation allows us to deliver the right message at the right time.

---

# Organic Growth

A large part of our strategy focuses on **long-term discoverability**.

This includes:

• search engine optimization
• app store optimization
• content strategy
• keyword research

By improving how people find our products in search and app stores, we build sustainable organic growth.

---

# Web Platforms

Our websites play an important role in product discovery and education.

They help users understand financial products, compare options, and start their journey into digital banking.

The web team works closely with product and marketing to constantly improve user experience and conversion.

---

# Data-Driven Growth

Every decision we make is supported by data.

Our marketing analytics team focuses on:

• funnel analysis
• attribution modeling
• experiment evaluation
• performance monitoring

This allows us to test new ideas quickly and scale the ones that work.

---

# AI in Sales

We are also building AI-powered communication tools.

These include:

• conversational chat systems

These tools help us communicate with customers at scale while keeping interactions personalized.

---

# Referral & Partnerships

Word-of-mouth remains one of the most powerful growth channels.

Our referral programs allow users to invite friends using personal links.

We also collaborate with creators, influencers, and affiliate partners who introduce our products to new audiences.

---

# Human Support at Scale

Alongside digital channels, a large telesales team supports user communication and product adoption.

They help users complete key steps, answer questions, and guide customers through financial decisions.

This combination of digital and human interaction creates a stronger customer experience.
